A little about myself...

Through Middle and High School I indulged my natural talent for music and writing, while exploring my interest in sciences. I strove to learn as much as I could about those subjects, both in and out of school, because they spoke to me. While working as a sessionist3 after high school, I held a day job at a local golf course. My employer saw the makings of golf course superintendent in me: Strong communications skills, a knack for problem solving, and a mind for science. When I decided it was a career I wanted to pursue, he endorsed my application to Michigan State University's Turf Management Program.
